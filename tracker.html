    Sync Your Assignments

    Use the Chrome extension to automatically pull assignments from Brightspace — no passwords stored, no credentials shared.

    1
    Install the Chrome Extension

    Open chrome://extensions, enable Developer Mode, click "Load unpacked" and select the chrome-extension folder from the project.

    2
    Log in to Brightspace

    Open your university's Brightspace and log in normally. The extension uses your existing session — nothing is stored.

    3
    Click "Sync Assignments"

    Click the extension icon and hit Sync. It scans your courses, reads assignments and due dates, and sends them here.

    Your Brightspace credentials are never sent to our servers. The extension reads your assignments directly from your browser where you're already logged in.
