    How it works

    Four steps. Under a minute. No copy-pasting assignment instructions.

    Sync

    Install the Chrome extension, open Brightspace, click sync. It pulls every assignment, rubric, and due date across all your courses. Takes about 10 seconds.

    Pick

    Open the tracker. Your assignments are sorted by urgency. The overdue ones are at the top (sorry). Click "AI Help" on whichever one you need.

    Generate

    The system reads the full instructions and rubric, figures out what format the prof wants, and writes the whole thing. If it says "2000-word essay in APA format about supply chain ethics," that's what you get. If it says "create a pivot table in the attached Excel file," you get numbered steps.

    Download

    DOCX for written assignments. PPTX for slide decks. Open it, review it, make it yours, submit it. The writing already passes as human because the humanizer runs on everything before it reaches you.
